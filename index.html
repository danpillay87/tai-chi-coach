STILL

A calm, on-device posture coach. Stand back so your whole body is in frame, facing the camera. Pick a posture, and STILL guides you into it — a glowing target to match, and a quiet cue for the one thing to fix. Move slowly, like tai chi.

Nothing you film is recorded or uploaded. Frames are read and discarded; only the numbers stay on this device.

On-device · frames never saved or uploaded · numbers only

Postures

STILL guides you into the selected posture and scores how close you are. Capture your own by getting into the pose and pressing Capture new — a 3-second countdown gives you time to settle. Your captures are the reference to match. (Two sample postures ship so it works out of the box.)

Data

Everything lives on this device. Reference postures are stored as normalised numbers — never images.
